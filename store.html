sneak peek into how i create my artworks

every painting passes through various stages, from the idea,to the sketch, to building the canvas and laying the paint. these stages are always rough and unpredictable, you never know how it's going to turn out, but the goal is to keep going until it comes alive, many times i find myself lost in the process, questioning the idea but i always try to push through the moment of doubt and replace it with curiosity and playfulness, allowing the painting to take its own shape and form. paintings can be time consuming, but i try to keep the process fun and light, experimenting with different techniques and approaches, learning from mistakes and unexpected outcomes.
